## **2. What is Beehiiv?**

Beehiiv is a newsletter platform designed for creators building audience-driven businesses. The platform combines newsletter writing and sending, website/blog publishing with SEO optimization, referral programs for viral growth, recommendation network (cross-promotion between newsletters), paid subscriptions and monetization, and audience analytics with growth metrics.

Unlike ConvertKit (which is an email marketing platform serving creators), Beehiiv is a newsletter platform—the distinction matters. Beehiiv's strengths are in publishing, growth, and monetization. ConvertKit's strengths are in email automation, sequences, and commerce. Choose based on whether you're building a newsletter business (Beehiiv) or a creator business that includes email (ConvertKit).

## **4. Key Features**

### **4.1 Referral Program - Growth Engine**

\[SCREENSHOT: Referral program dashboard showing milestones and subscriber growth\]

The built-in referral program lets you set milestone rewards (refer 3 friends → get exclusive content, refer 10 → get premium access). Each subscriber gets a unique referral link. The program gamifies list growth in ways that organic signup alone can't match. Our referral program generated 23% of total subscriber growth during testing.

### **4.2 Recommendation Network**

\[SCREENSHOT: Recommendation setup showing cross-promotion with other newsletters\]

After a reader subscribes, Beehiiv recommends other newsletters they might enjoy—and vice versa. Your newsletter gets recommended to subscribers of similar newsletters, creating a cross-promotion network. We gained 400+ subscribers through the recommendation network over four months—free growth from audience overlap.

### **4.3 Web Publishing & SEO**

Newsletters publish as web pages with SEO optimization—meta tags, clean URLs, structured data. Unlike Substack (which hosts on substack.com subdomain), Beehiiv supports custom domains. Our newsletter posts ranked in Google search, driving organic discovery beyond email-only distribution.

### **4.4 Monetization**

Paid subscriptions (Stripe integration), Beehiiv Ad Network (monetize through ads placed in your newsletter), and sponsorship marketplace. The Ad Network is unique—Beehiiv matches advertisers with newsletters based on audience, providing a revenue stream without the creator doing sponsor outreach.

### **4.5 Analytics**

Growth metrics, engagement analytics, referral tracking, subscriber sources, and revenue dashboards. The analytics focus on growth (subscriber acquisition channels, referral performance) rather than marketing metrics (automation performance, lead scoring).
