## **1. Introduction**

Dixa (pronounced "dik-sa") is a conversational customer service platform that routes all channels—chat, email, phone, and social—through a single, intelligent routing engine. After three months evaluating Dixa with a 12-person team, I found a platform that handles multi-channel routing more elegantly than Zendesk while lacking the enterprise depth and ecosystem that Zendesk provides.

Founded in 2015 in Copenhagen, Denmark, Dixa serves primarily European and mid-market organizations. The platform's intelligent routing assigns conversations to the best available agent based on skills, availability, and customer history—not just a simple queue. The routing quality is genuinely superior to Zendesk's default routing.

The platform combines live chat, phone (browser-based VOIP), email, and social messaging in a unified agent view. The agent experience is clean and conversational—closer to Intercom's messaging feel than Zendesk's ticket management. For mid-market teams wanting better routing than Zendesk's defaults without Zendesk's complexity, Dixa fills a genuine niche.

## **2. Verdict**

### **Overall Rating: 3.7/5**

Dixa serves mid-market support teams well with elegant routing and a clean agent experience. The platform lacks Zendesk's enterprise depth and Intercom's AI sophistication but provides a more modern, routing-focused alternative for teams of 10-50 agents. The European headquarters and EU data centers serve GDPR-focused organizations.

**Best For:** Mid-market European support teams (10-50 agents) wanting intelligent multi-channel routing.

**Not Recommended For:** Enterprise organizations, teams needing deep AI, or very small teams.
