## **1. Introduction**

Drift isn't a customer support tool—it's a conversational marketing and sales platform that uses chatbots, live chat, and AI to convert anonymous website visitors into qualified sales meetings. After three months evaluating Drift for a B2B SaaS company, I found it effective at its specific mission: turning website traffic into pipeline. Our demo request conversion rate increased 35% with Drift's chatbot qualifying visitors and booking meetings directly.

Drift was founded in 2015 by David Cancel and Elias Torres, acquired by Vista Equity Partners in 2022, and subsequently merged with Salesloft in 2024. The merger creates an interesting positioning: Drift's conversational marketing capabilities now complement Salesloft's sales engagement platform.

The platform excels when high-value B2B website visitors need immediate engagement. A visitor on your pricing page at 2 PM should be talking to a sales rep, not filling out a form and waiting 24 hours. Drift makes that instant connection happen through AI chatbot qualification and real-time routing to available reps.

## **2. Key Features & Verdict**

**AI Chatbot:** Qualifies visitors by asking about company size, role, and use case. Routes qualified leads to available reps for live chat or books meetings directly. Our chatbot qualified 23% of pricing page visitors as sales-ready.

**Live Chat with Routing:** When the chatbot qualifies a visitor, Drift routes them to the appropriate sales rep for live conversation. The routing considers territory, industry specialization, and real-time availability.

**Meeting Scheduling:** Built-in calendar integration lets visitors book meetings directly from chat—no separate Calendly link needed. The frictionless booking increased our meeting booking rate by 40%.

**Account-Based Targeting:** Identifies companies visiting your site (through IP intelligence) and triggers personalized experiences for target accounts. Enterprise visitors see different chatbot messaging than SMB visitors.

**Revenue Insights:** Tracks pipeline and revenue attributed to conversational interactions. Our attribution data showed $180,000 in pipeline generated through Drift conversations in three months.

### **Overall Rating: 3.8/5**

Drift is the best conversational marketing platform for B2B companies with sales teams ready to engage website visitors. The pipeline attribution is measurable and compelling. The pricing ($2,500+/month) requires the pipeline contribution to justify the investment—which for B2B companies with $10K+ deal sizes, it typically does.

**Best For:** B2B SaaS and enterprise companies wanting to convert website traffic into sales pipeline through conversational engagement.

**Not Recommended For:** Customer support (use Intercom or Zendesk), small businesses without sales teams, or companies with low website traffic.
