## **1. Introduction**

Gorgias is the only help desk I've used where an agent can view a customer's order, issue a refund, cancel a subscription, and update their shipping address—all without leaving the support ticket. After four months managing 1,500+ tickets monthly for a DTC Shopify brand, I found that this e-commerce-specific depth transforms support productivity in ways that generic help desks (Zendesk, Freshdesk) can't match.

Founded in 2015 in San Francisco, Gorgias serves over 12,000 e-commerce brands including Steve Madden, Ellesse, and Marine Layer. The platform integrates deeply with Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento—pulling order data, customer history, and subscription information directly into the support interface.

The platform's per-ticket pricing is unusual and worth understanding. Instead of charging per agent (like Zendesk), Gorgias charges per ticket volume. This means adding support agents doesn't increase costs—only ticket volume does. For e-commerce brands that scale agents seasonally (holiday rushes), this model can save significant money.

## **2. Key Features & Verdict**

**Shopify Deep Integration:** View orders, issue refunds, cancel/modify orders, update shipping, manage subscriptions, apply discounts—all from the ticket sidebar. Our agents saved 3-4 minutes per ticket by eliminating Shopify admin tab-switching.

**Macros with Order Data:** Templated responses that dynamically insert order-specific data (tracking number, delivery date, order total). Our "where's my order?" response auto-populated tracking information, reducing that ticket category to a 15-second interaction.

**Revenue Tracking:** Gorgias tracks revenue generated through support interactions (upsells, saved orders, prevented cancellations). Our support team generated $12,000/month in attributable revenue through proactive engagement.

**Multi-Channel:** Email, chat, social (Facebook, Instagram), SMS, and phone—all in a unified inbox with Shopify context on every conversation.

**Automation Rules:** Auto-tag, auto-assign, and auto-respond based on ticket content. Our rules handled 30% of tickets (order tracking, return policy inquiries) without agent involvement.

### **Overall Rating: 4.0/5**

Gorgias is the best help desk for Shopify-based e-commerce brands. The store integration depth, per-ticket pricing, and revenue tracking create genuine e-commerce value. For non-e-commerce businesses, Gorgias provides no advantage—use Zendesk or Freshdesk instead.

**Best For:** Shopify/BigCommerce brands handling product inquiries, order management, and customer support.

**Not Recommended For:** Non-e-commerce businesses or very small stores under 200 tickets/month.
