## **2. What is Klaviyo?**

\[VISUAL: Klaviyo's e-commerce data model showing product, customer, and behavioral data flowing into email/SMS\]

Klaviyo is an email and SMS marketing platform built specifically for e-commerce. The platform ingests data from your online store (products, orders, customer behavior, website visits), enriches it with predictive analytics (lifetime value, churn risk, next purchase prediction), and uses it to power personalized email and SMS campaigns.

What fundamentally separates Klaviyo from Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, or Brevo is the depth of e-commerce data integration. Klaviyo doesn't just know that a customer bought something—it knows what they bought, when, how much they spent lifetime, what products they've browsed since, whether they're likely to purchase again, and when. This data powers segmentation ("customers who bought Product A but haven't purchased in 60 days with a lifetime value above $200") and personalization (dynamic product recommendations based on individual browsing and purchase patterns).

The platform combines email marketing, SMS marketing, and reviews/forms into a unified system. Flows (automated sequences triggered by behavior) and campaigns (one-time sends to segments) operate on the same data with the same segmentation engine.

## **4. Key Features**

### **4.1 Flows - Behavioral Email Automation for E-Commerce**

\[SCREENSHOT: Flow builder showing an abandoned cart sequence with conditional splits based on cart value\]

Klaviyo's flows are automated email/SMS sequences triggered by customer behavior. The flow builder provides conditional splits, time delays, A/B testing, and dynamic content—all designed for e-commerce use cases.

Our highest-revenue flow was the abandoned cart sequence. When a customer added products to cart but didn't check out within one hour, Klaviyo sent a personalized reminder showing the exact products in their cart, with prices, images, and a direct link to checkout. If they didn't convert, a second email 24 hours later included a 10% discount for first-time buyers (conditional: only if they'd never purchased before). If they still didn't convert, a final email 48 hours later created urgency with limited stock messaging (conditional: only if the product had inventory below a threshold). This three-email flow recovered $4,200/month in otherwise-lost revenue.

Other high-performing flows included: welcome series (5 emails introducing the brand, educating on products, and offering a first-purchase incentive—converted 12% of new subscribers), post-purchase (thank you, shipping updates, usage tips, review request—timed to estimated delivery date), browse abandonment (targeting visitors who viewed products but didn't add to cart—generated $1,800/month), and win-back (re-engaging customers who hadn't purchased in 90+ days with personalized product recommendations based on their purchase history).

### **4.2 Segmentation - E-Commerce-Grade Targeting**

\[SCREENSHOT: Segment builder showing purchase behavior, browsing, and predictive data conditions\]

Klaviyo's segmentation is where the e-commerce data depth becomes tangible. Segments can combine purchase history (bought specific product, total orders, average order value, last purchase date), browsing behavior (viewed specific products, category, frequency), email engagement (opened, clicked, converted), predictive data (predicted next order date, churn risk score, predicted lifetime value), and custom properties.

Our most effective segment: "Customers who purchased from Collection X in the last 90 days AND have a predicted lifetime value above $300 AND haven't purchased in 45+ days AND have opened at least 1 email in the last 30 days." This segment received a personalized "new arrivals in Collection X" email with dynamic product recommendations. The campaign to this segment achieved a 6.8% conversion rate versus 1.2% for our general list—demonstrating the power of behavioral segmentation.

### **4.3 Predictive Analytics - AI for E-Commerce**

\[SCREENSHOT: Customer profile showing predicted lifetime value, churn risk, and next order prediction\]

Klaviyo's predictive analytics calculate three metrics per customer: expected date of next order, predicted customer lifetime value, and churn risk probability. These predictions are based on each customer's purchase history compared to patterns across your entire customer base.

We used churn risk scoring to trigger win-back automations proactively—reaching customers showing churn signals before they fully lapsed. The proactive approach retained an estimated 8% of at-risk customers who would have churned without intervention.

### **4.4 SMS Marketing**

Klaviyo's SMS adds text messaging as a channel alongside email. SMS campaigns, flows, and automation support conversation-style messaging, MMS (images/GIFs), and compliance tools (opt-in management, quiet hours). Our SMS campaigns achieved 25% click-through rates—dramatically higher than email (3-4%)—making SMS the higher-converting channel for time-sensitive promotions.

### **4.5 Revenue Attribution**

Klaviyo tracks revenue attributed to every email and SMS. Our dashboard showed exactly which flows, campaigns, and individual messages generated purchases. The $180,000+ in attributable revenue over six months gave our marketing team concrete ROI data for budget discussions—something general email tools provide less precisely.

## **6. Klaviyo Cons**

### **E-Commerce Only** - Klaviyo is built for online stores. B2B companies, SaaS, content creators, and non-e-commerce businesses get no advantage over general email platforms. The e-commerce focus is a strength for stores and a limitation for everyone else.

### **Pricing Is Premium** - At 35,000 contacts, Klaviyo costs ~$460/month. Mailchimp Standard costs ~$270. ActiveCampaign Plus costs ~$209. Brevo costs ~$65. The pricing premium requires the revenue attribution to justify—which for successful e-commerce brands, it does.

### **Complexity for Simple Needs** - If you just want to send a newsletter to your customer list, Klaviyo's data depth and flow complexity are overkill. Mailchimp or Brevo handle simple campaigns with less overhead.

### **Learning Curve for Advanced Flows** - Building sophisticated conditional flows with A/B tests and dynamic content takes time. The pre-built templates help, but customizing them for your specific business requires learning the platform's data model.

### **SMS Costs Add Up** - SMS is billed separately per message ($0.01-0.02/SMS in the US). High-volume SMS campaigns to large segments create significant additional costs beyond the base subscription.
