## **2. What is Lemlist? Understanding the Platform**

\[VISUAL: Lemlist's evolution from email personalization tool to multi-channel outreach platform\]

Lemlist is a cold email outreach and sales engagement platform designed to help B2B sales teams send personalized cold emails at scale. The platform combines an email warmup tool (Lemwarm), a B2B contact database, multi-channel sequence builder (email, LinkedIn, phone), and AI-powered content generation.

The platform's founding innovation—custom image personalization—remains its core differentiator. While competitors like Instantly or Mailshake let you personalize text ({firstName}, {company}), Lemlist lets you dynamically generate images that include the prospect's name, company logo, website screenshot, or custom text overlaid on branded templates. The visual impact in crowded inboxes is significant—our image-personalized emails had 35% higher open rates than text-only variants.

Since the image personalization breakthrough, Lemlist has evolved into a broader outreach platform. The multi-channel sequencer orchestrates email, LinkedIn connection requests, LinkedIn messages, and phone calls in coordinated cadences. The Lemwarm email warmup tool builds sender reputation before campaigns launch. And the B2B database (acquired through Lemlist's purchase of Upscale) provides contact data for prospecting.

The platform serves a sweet spot between simple cold email tools (Instantly, Mailshake) and enterprise sales engagement platforms (Outreach, Salesloft). It's more sophisticated than the budget options but more accessible and affordable than the enterprise tier.

## **4. Key Features Deep Dive**

### **4.1 Image Personalization - The Killer Feature**

\[SCREENSHOT: Email composer showing a custom image with prospect's name dynamically inserted\]

This is why Lemlist exists and why teams choose it over cheaper alternatives. The custom image engine generates unique images for each recipient by overlaying personalization variables onto template images.

We tested extensively across three personalization strategies. Strategy one: a whiteboard image with the prospect's name and a key metric about their company. Strategy two: a laptop screen showing the prospect's website with annotations highlighting where our product would help. Strategy three: a personalized video thumbnail showing the prospect's name and company logo. All three significantly outperformed text-only emails, with the laptop screenshot strategy producing the highest engagement (42% open rate, 11% reply rate).

The image editor is straightforward—upload a template image, add text layers with personalization variables, position them visually, and preview with sample data. Each email recipient sees their unique version. The images render as embedded pictures in the email, not as attachments, which is important for deliverability.

**What's Missing:** Creating high-quality template images requires design skills. Lemlist provides templates, but the best-performing images are custom-designed for your brand and use case. Plan for 2-3 hours of design work per template.

### **4.2 Email Warmup (Lemwarm) - Deliverability Foundation**

\[SCREENSHOT: Lemwarm dashboard showing reputation score and warmup progress\]

Lemwarm is Lemlist's email warmup tool, and it's essential for cold email success. The tool sends and receives emails between real accounts in Lemwarm's network, gradually building your domain's sender reputation before you launch campaigns.

We warmed our sending domains for three weeks before launching our first campaigns. The warmup involved sending 20-40 emails per day through Lemwarm, gradually increasing volume while monitoring reputation scores. By the time we started real campaigns, our domains had established reputation, and our initial deliverability was strong—93% inbox placement on our first campaign.

**Pro Tip:** Never skip warmup. We tested sending from an un-warmed domain: 45% of emails landed in spam. After three weeks of Lemwarm: 93% inbox placement. The warmup period is non-negotiable for cold email.

### **4.3 Multi-Channel Sequences - Beyond Email**

\[SCREENSHOT: Sequence builder showing email, LinkedIn, and phone steps in a coordinated cadence\]

Lemlist's multi-channel sequencer (Multichannel Expert) orchestrates outreach across email, LinkedIn, and phone in coordinated campaigns. Each step can be automated (email sends) or manual with prompts (LinkedIn connection requests, phone calls).

The LinkedIn integration was transformative for our outreach. After sending two emails without reply, a LinkedIn connection request provided a second touchpoint that felt natural rather than pestering. Our data showed that 40% of our booked meetings came from prospects who didn't reply to email but engaged after the LinkedIn touch. Multi-channel isn't just a feature—it's a necessary evolution as email-only outreach becomes less effective.

### **4.4 B2B Contact Database**

Lemlist includes a B2B database for prospecting—searchable by role, industry, company size, and location. The database is smaller than Apollo's (275M+) or ZoomInfo's but sufficient for teams that need basic prospecting within their outreach tool. We used it to supplement our primary prospecting source (Apollo) for specific niche searches.

### **4.5 AI Email Writing**

Lemlist's AI generates email copy based on your product description, target persona, and campaign goal. The quality is usable as a starting point—about 50% of AI-generated emails needed minimal editing, 30% needed significant revision, and 20% were off-target. We used the AI to generate first drafts and A/B test variants, then refined winning versions manually.

## **6. Lemlist Cons**

\[VISUAL: Cons summary infographic\]

### **Pricing Is Premium for Cold Email**

At $55-129/user/month, Lemlist costs 2-4x more than Instantly ($30/user) or Smartlead ($39/user). The image personalization justifies the premium for teams that use it—but teams doing text-only outreach get better value elsewhere.

### **No Built-In CRM**

Lemlist handles outreach but not pipeline management. You need a separate CRM (Pipedrive, HubSpot) for deal tracking after prospects reply. The CRM integrations work but add another tool to your stack.

### **B2B Database Is Smaller Than Apollo's**

If you're using Lemlist for prospecting AND outreach, the database is a nice bonus but not a replacement for Apollo or ZoomInfo. Most teams still need a separate prospecting data source.

### **Learning Curve for Image Personalization**

Creating effective personalized images requires design sense, template creation skills, and testing. The feature is powerful but not instant—plan for experimentation time.

### **LinkedIn Automation Has Platform Risk**

LinkedIn connection requests and messages through automation tools exist in a gray area of LinkedIn's terms of service. Aggressive LinkedIn automation can result in account restrictions. Use conservative daily limits and genuine personalization.
