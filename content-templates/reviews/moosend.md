## **1. Introduction**

Moosend's pitch is simple: capable email marketing at pricing that undercuts nearly every competitor. At $9/month for 500 subscribers (compared to Mailchimp's $13 and ActiveCampaign's $15), Moosend provides email campaigns, automation workflows, landing pages, and forms—the core of what most small businesses need.

After three months with a 5,000-subscriber list, I found Moosend delivers on the value promise. The email builder is clean and functional. The automation handles standard workflows (welcome series, abandoned cart, date-based). The landing pages are adequate for lead capture. The platform doesn't match ActiveCampaign's automation depth or Mailchimp's template variety, but for businesses where every dollar matters, Moosend provides genuine capability at the lowest cost tier.

Moosend was acquired by Sitecore in 2021, bringing enterprise resources and stability. The platform serves primarily small businesses and startups in Europe and internationally.

## **2. Key Features & Verdict**

**Email Builder:** Drag-and-drop with 70+ templates. The builder is intuitive and produces clean, mobile-responsive emails. Template variety doesn't match Mailchimp (100+) but covers common use cases.

**Automation:** Visual workflow builder with triggers (signup, cart abandonment, date, custom event), conditions, and actions. Handles standard automations well. Complex conditional branching is limited compared to ActiveCampaign.

**Landing Pages:** Simple builder with templates for lead capture and product promotion. Functional for basic landing page needs.

**Reporting:** Campaign metrics (opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes), automation performance, and basic revenue tracking. Adequate for small business reporting.

**Pricing at Scale:** 5,000 subscribers costs $32/month (vs Mailchimp $52, ActiveCampaign $49). 25,000 subscribers costs $160/month (vs Mailchimp $270, ActiveCampaign $209). The savings are consistent across all sizes.

### **Overall Rating: 3.6/5**

Moosend is the most affordable capable email marketing platform. It delivers 70% of what Mailchimp offers at 60% of the price. For budget-constrained small businesses where email marketing needs are straightforward (campaigns, basic automations, landing pages), Moosend provides genuine value without the premium pricing of market leaders.

**Best For:** Budget-focused SMBs wanting capable email marketing at the lowest price.

**Not Recommended For:** Teams needing advanced automation, the best templates, or e-commerce-specific features.
