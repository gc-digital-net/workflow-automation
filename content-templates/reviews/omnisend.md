## **1. Introduction**

Omnisend is the Klaviyo alternative for e-commerce stores that want strong email and SMS capabilities without Klaviyo's premium pricing. After four months with a 12,000-contact Shopify store, I found Omnisend delivers 80% of Klaviyo's e-commerce email capability at 60% of the price—a value proposition that matters for independent stores where every marketing dollar competes with inventory and advertising spend.

Founded in 2014 in Lithuania, Omnisend serves over 100,000 e-commerce brands. The platform combines email marketing, SMS, push notifications, and pre-built e-commerce automations (abandoned cart, welcome, post-purchase, browse abandonment) in a single platform with native Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce integrations.

The pricing advantage is real: our 12,000-contact Shopify store paid $115/month on Omnisend Pro versus the $150/month Klaviyo would charge. The feature gap—primarily in predictive analytics and advanced segmentation—was acceptable for our store's needs.

## **2. Key Features & Verdict**

**E-Commerce Automations:** Pre-built workflows for all standard e-commerce sequences. The abandoned cart flow recovered $2,400/month. Welcome series converted 15% of new subscribers. The automations set up in hours with minimal customization.

**Email + SMS:** Unified email and SMS from the same platform with shared segments and automation. SMS messages within email automations create multi-channel touchpoints. SMS click rates (18%) significantly outperformed email (3%).

**Product Recommendations:** Dynamic product blocks in emails based on purchase history and browsing. The recommendations are simpler than Klaviyo's but functional for standard product suggestion scenarios.

**Segmentation:** Purchase-based and behavior-based segments. "Customers who bought from Category X in the last 60 days but haven't purchased since" creates useful targeting. Advanced predictive segments (lifetime value, churn risk) are limited compared to Klaviyo.

**Campaign Builder:** Drag-and-drop email builder with e-commerce-specific blocks (product listings, discount codes, reviews). The builder is intuitive and the templates are designed for online stores.

### **Overall Rating: 3.9/5**

Omnisend is the best mid-price e-commerce email platform—more affordable than Klaviyo with stronger e-commerce features than Mailchimp. For Shopify stores that need behavioral automation and SMS but can't justify Klaviyo's premium, Omnisend provides excellent value.

**Best For:** E-commerce stores wanting email + SMS at moderate pricing.

**Not Recommended For:** Non-e-commerce, stores needing the deepest analytics (use Klaviyo), or budget-minimum needs (use Brevo free).
