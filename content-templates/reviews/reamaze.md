## **1. Introduction**

Re:amaze is a customer support platform built for e-commerce and online businesses. After three months on a small Shopify store, I found it a solid middle ground between Freshdesk (cheaper but not e-commerce-specific) and Gorgias (more Shopify-specific but more expensive). The platform handles email, chat, social, and SMS in a unified inbox with Shopify order data accessible within conversations.

Re:amaze was created by GoDaddy (acquired in 2020), which provides stability but hasn't driven rapid innovation. The platform serves smaller e-commerce stores that need more than Freshdesk's generic ticketing but don't need Gorgias's deep Shopify integration at Gorgias's higher pricing.

The platform includes a customizable FAQ/help center, live chat with automated responses, and basic chatbot functionality. For small e-commerce operations (under 1,000 tickets/month), Re:amaze provides adequate multi-channel support at competitive pricing.

## **2. Verdict**

### **Overall Rating: 3.6/5**

Re:amaze is a capable, affordable e-commerce support platform. It doesn't match Gorgias's Shopify depth or Zendesk's enterprise power, but for small stores wanting multi-channel support with e-commerce context, it provides solid value at accessible pricing.

**Best For:** Small e-commerce stores wanting affordable multi-channel support with order integration.

**Not Recommended For:** Large support teams, non-e-commerce businesses, or teams wanting the most advanced features.
