## **1. Introduction**

Stacker builds internal tools and client portals from existing data sources—Airtable, Google Sheets, Salesforce, and SQL databases. The platform positions between Softr (Airtable portals) and Retool (developer internal tools) by serving operations teams who want more than a portal but less than a full development platform.

After three months building an internal CRM and client portal, I found Stacker delivers functional business applications from existing data. The user authentication and role-based access control work well for both internal and client-facing use cases. The app builder is more capable than Pory or basic Softr but less powerful than Retool or Bubble.

The platform's strength is data source flexibility. Connecting to Airtable, Google Sheets, Salesforce, and SQL means you can build portals on top of whatever system your data already lives in—without migrating data or learning a new database.

## **2. Verdict**

### **Overall Rating: 3.5/5**

Stacker serves operations teams needing portals from multiple data sources with user authentication. It bridges the gap between simple portal builders and full development platforms. The multi-data-source support and role-based access serve real business needs.

**Best For:** Operations teams needing authenticated portals from Airtable, Sheets, or Salesforce.

**Not Recommended For:** Complex applications or teams wanting the most polished experience.
