Founded in 2013 in Szczecin, Poland, Tidio serves over 300,000 businesses—primarily e-commerce stores on Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. The Lyro AI chatbot, launched in 2023, resolves customer questions using your help content, similar to Intercom's Fin but at dramatically lower pricing.

The platform excels at the intersection of sales chat and support chat that e-commerce requires. ## **2. Key Features & Verdict**

**Lyro AI:** AI chatbot trained on your help content. Resolved 60% of common e-commerce inquiries (shipping, returns, sizing, availability). Less sophisticated than Intercom's Fin but remarkably capable at $29/month.

**Live Chat:** Real-time visitor monitoring, proactive messages based on page and behavior, typing preview, and visitor information. The chat experience is optimized for e-commerce—showing cart contents, browsing history, and visit count.

**E-Commerce Integrations:** Deep Shopify and WooCommerce integration showing order history, cart contents, and product recommendations within the chat.
